* Jungle Bird                                     :cocktail:tiki:rum:campari:

[[./img/jungle-bird.jpg]]

- 1 1/2 oz blackstrap rum (a black, navy rum, 40% ABV)
- 3/4 oz campari
- 4 oz pineapple juice
- 1/2 oz lime juice
- 1/2 oz simple syrup

Shake everything, pour unstrained into a double old-fashioned glass.
